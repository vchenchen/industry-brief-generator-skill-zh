## When The Industry Is Too Broad

```text
使用 $industry-brief-generator-zh。

输入具体行业：制造业
输入具体市场：全球
输入重点关注内容：行业热点
输入排除内容：泛财经、自媒体
输入最终用途：公众号
```

Expected behavior: ask the user to narrow the industry, for example to chemical, packaging materials, semiconductor equipment, machine tools, or industrial automation.
